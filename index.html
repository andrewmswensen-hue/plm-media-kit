About Peter Lohmann

Peter Lohmann is one of the most trusted and influential voices in property management today.

His newsletter and podcast reach over 20,000 property management company owners, vendors, and real estate investors every week.

He speaks at leading industry events including NARPM National & Broker/Owner and LeadSimple University, and has been featured on top podcasts including Profitable Property Management and Top Floor.

As CEO of a 700+ unit property management company and co-founder of Crane, an exclusive online community for PM operators, Peter combines real-world operator experience with polished, data-driven industry insights.

Advertising Philosophy

Brand equity, not banner noise.

Build brand equity, not just clicks

Advertising with Peter is an effective, low-friction way to position your brand as a trusted name in property management. The audience is highly engaged, niche-specific, and pays attention to thoughtful, well-placed messages.

Native ads, not banner noise

No flashy, interruptive banners. Ads blend into the design and tone of the newsletters. They stand out because they resonate with an engaged audience, not because they scream for attention.

Who this is (and isn't) for

This isn't Google or Facebook ads. If you're after quick conversions or performance-style metrics, this may not be the fit. Sponsorships are built to grow long-term brand awareness, credibility, and trust with a niche, high-value audience.

Three ways to show up.

Newsletter

Twice-weekly insights (Tuesday & Friday) on PM news, events, and Peter's perspectives.
